Instructions

Step By Step on Using Phys-Trax

Initial Set Up: USE GOOGLE CHROME!!!


  1. Consult the Bangle.js 2 'Getting Started' website for more detailed instructions here
  2. To Charge: Position the watch so the button is on the right side. The charger cable should exit from the right so avoid shorting the watch:

  3. Do not use it while charging!
  4. Press the button on the side to start the Bangle
  5. The touchscreen is locked by default, press the button again to unlock the screen
  6. This is also how to wake the screen when it dims mid-use
  7. Pressing again will take you to the App Launcher which displays the apps currently downloaded on the Bangle

Using the PhysTrax App

  1. Scroll to the app PhysTrax after opening the App Launcher
  2. In PhysTrax, press the side button to start the heart rate measurement. Press once and wait for the buzz to confirm the input
    1. It will display “Measuring..” for about 30 seconds before a reading shows on the watch
    2. If the screen dims, you can press the button again to wake up. It will not stop heart rate measurement
  3. With the screen awake, press the button again to stop heart rate measurement
  4. From there, connect to the Bangle App Loader -> My Apps -> PhysTrax. There should be a save button that would allow you to save the CSV file from your measurement session
  5. The connection to your device requires you to stay in the vicinity of your device however it needs to be connected to a device to download the CSV file
  6. There is limited memory on the device: Don’t take more than an hour of measurements in one session

After data is collected:

  1. Navigate to 'Solo Data' and choose your specific .csv files for upload.
  2. Upon successful upload, your heart rate and heart rate variability graphs will be visible for review.
  3. If you are comfortable with sharing your physiological data for educational purposes, kindly complete the accompanying survey under 'Class Data'
  4. Explore and analyze your class's collective results!

Useful Settings:

  1. Connect to the Bangle App Loader here
      2. After successful connection, click on the More.. Tab to set the Time for the Bangle
  2. Upon successful upload, your heart rate and heart rate variability graphs will be visible for review.
  3. In the Settings app on the Bangle, navigate to System -> LCD to set more wake settings, like “Wake on Tap” or “Wake on Twist”
